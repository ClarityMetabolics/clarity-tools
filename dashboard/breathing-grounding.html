Simple Deep Breathing
Reset in 2 minutes
When you're feeling stressed or scattered, taking a few slow, deep breaths can help you feel more settled. This is the simplest way to calm your nervous system.
How to do it:
  • 1Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 counts
  • 2Hold for 2 counts
  • 3Breathe out slowly through your mouth for 6 counts
  • 4Repeat 5-10 times

Feel Your Feet
Connect to the ground
This simple technique helps you feel more grounded and present by focusing on the physical connection between your body and the earth beneath you.
How to do it:
  • 1Sit or stand with your feet flat on the floor
  • 2Take off your shoes if possible
  • 3Notice the weight of your feet
  • 4Feel the connection to the ground
  • 5Stay here for 1-2 minutes

Hand on Heart
Self-soothing touch
Sometimes a gentle touch can be comforting when you're feeling anxious or upset. This simple gesture can help you feel more calm and connected to yourself.
How to do it:
  • 1Place one or both hands on your chest
  • 2Feel the warmth of your hands
  • 3Notice your heartbeat
  • 4Breathe slowly and gently
  • 5Stay here as long as feels good
