‚ö° EMERGENCY PROTOCOL (Use Immediately)
  1. STOP - Put down your phone/device. Step away from the trigger.
  2. BREATHE - 4 counts in, 6 counts out. Repeat 5 times minimum.
  3. GROUND - Name 5 things you can see, 4 you can touch, 3 you can hear.
  4. CONNECT - Place hand on heart. Say: "I am safe right now."
  5. CHOOSE - What would a regulated version of me do next?
Remember: Your nervous system thinks there's a tiger in the room. There isn't. You are safe to slow down and regulate.
ü´Å EMERGENCY BREATHING TECHNIQUES
Box Breathing (for panic/overwhelm):
  1. Breathe in for 4 counts
  2. Hold for 4 counts
  3. Breathe out for 4 counts
  4. Hold empty for 4 counts
  5. Repeat 8-10 times
Calming Breath (for anger/activation):
  1. Breathe in through nose for 4 counts
  2. Hold gently for 4 counts
  3. Breathe out through mouth for 6 counts
  4. Natural pause for 2 counts
  5. Repeat until calm
Heart Coherence (for emotional stability):
  1. Place hand on heart
  2. Breathe into your heart space (5 counts in)
  3. Breathe out through your heart (5 counts out)
  4. Think of something you're grateful for
  5. Continue for 2-3 minutes
‚öì GROUNDING TECHNIQUES
5-4-3-2-1 Technique:
  • 5 things you can SEE around you
  • 4 things you can TOUCH (and touch them)
  • 3 things you can HEAR right now
  • 2 things you can SMELL
  • 1 thing you can TASTE
Physical Grounding:
  • Feel your feet on the ground
  • Press palms together firmly
  • Hold a cold object (ice cube, cold water)
  • Do 10 jumping jacks or push-ups
  • Splash cold water on your face
Spiritual Grounding:
  • Say a quick prayer or affirmation
  • Remember: "God is in control, not the chaos"
  • Picture yourself surrounded by divine light
  • Ask: "What would Love do right now?"
  • Connect with your deeper purpose
